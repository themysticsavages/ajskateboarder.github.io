QuikImpakt docs

This presentation:


is made almost completely from QuikImpakt. It summarizes drug information as visual data, as the slides show. This slideshow summarizes information about Aspirin.

What does it do?

Quikimpakt offers a summarized version of the top 7 abstracts found on PubMed regarding the search, a word mesh of the abstracts found on the PubMed documents found, a mesh with images of different forms of the searched drug on drugs.com, and the most popular drug structure found on PubChem.

What did you use?

QuikImpakt is made with Python 3 and the Flask framework, which powers many other large websites, like Netflix or Reddit.

The following APIs were used:

  • collections
  • pillow
  • beautifulsoup4
  • summarizer4u
  • photoroom
